Embed Google Calendar on your dashboard

1. On a computer, open Google Calendar. You can only get the code to embed in your website from a computer, not the Google Calendar app.

2. In the top right, click the Settings icon, and then click Settings.

3. On the left side of the screen, click the name of the calendar you want to embed.

4. Click the "Integrate calendar" section

5. Under the embed code, click Customize.

6. You are required to choose the following options in order for the calendar to fit on the dashboard:

✓ Width: 310
✓ Height: 200
✓ Default view: Agenda
✓ Make any other edits as you desire your calendar to appear.
